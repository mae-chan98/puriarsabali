Puri Arsa Bali is a tranquil and comfortable accommodation, offering an ideal retreat far from the hustle and bustle of city life. Nestled in a peaceful village in the southwest of Bali, we provide the perfect escape for those seeking serenity.

Our location is 25 km from Canggu, 15 km from the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, and just 2 km from the beautiful Kelating and Pasut Beaches popular wedding venues, along with the historic Puri Agung Kerambitan.

AT PURI ARSA BALI

Puri Arsa Bali offers accommodations with scenic pool views and a private balcony, located just 2.9 km from Kelating Beach. Guests have access to a terrace and free private parking. The rooms also feature a patio with serene garden views. Each unit comes equipped with air conditioning, a toaster, fridge, kettle, bidet, bathrobes, and a work desk. The private bathrooms include showers and slippers, while free WiFi is available throughout the property. Fresh bed linen and towels are provided for every guest. There is also an on-site bar for added convenience.

Guests can relax in the outdoor swimming pool or enjoy the beautiful garden area. The property is situated 13 km from the famous Tanah Lot Temple and 24 km from Petitenget Temple. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 33 km away from Puri Arsa Bali.
